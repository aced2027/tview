## 🖱️ How the Crosshair Works

### Automatic Activation
- **Hover over chart** → Crosshair appears
- **Move mouse** → Crosshair follows
- **Move away** → Crosshair disappears

### What You See
- **Vertical line** - Shows exact time on X-axis
- **Horizontal line** - Shows exact price on Y-axis
- **Dashed style** - Professional TradingView look
- **Labels** - Price and time values displayed

### Interaction
- **Hover** - See precise coordinates
- **Scroll wheel** - Zoom in/out
- **Click + drag** - Pan the chart
- **Double click** - Reset zoom
